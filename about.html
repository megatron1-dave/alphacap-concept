About Us

A Different Kind of
Investment Bank

Founded in London in 2016 and regulated by the FCA, ALPHACAP Partners was built on a single conviction: that clients in South-East Europe deserve the same calibre of truly independent, senior-level counsel as those served by the world's leading advisory firms.

We are not a volume business. We take fewer mandates so that every client receives genuine senior attention from inception to close.

ALPHACAP Partners specialises in Greece, Cyprus, and South-East Europe - markets that demand local intelligence, hard-won relationships, and the kind of experience that only comes from having operated through multiple cycles in the region.

Our team brings over two decades of transaction execution experience across Healthcare, Pharmaceuticals, Insurance, Banking, Telecommunications, Real Estate, Energy, Gaming, and FMCG. We have worked on both sides of the table - as investors, as business owners, and as bankers. That breadth informs every piece of advice we give.

We focus on special situations, restructurings, and growth investments, where our combination of deep regional knowledge and cross-border deal expertise creates the most value for clients.

Integrity

We say what we mean and mean what we say. Our reputation is our most important asset, and it is built on candour, not convenience.

Experienced Judgement

Decades of deal execution across complex, cross-border situations give our advice its weight. We have seen most things before.

Independent Advice

No conflicts, no house views, no product-pushing. We have no balance sheet to deploy and no secondary agenda. Just clear-headed counsel in your interest.

Long-term Relationships

We measure success in partnerships that endure long after a transaction closes. Many of our clients have worked with our principals for over a decade.
