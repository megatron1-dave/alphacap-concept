Careers

Work with Us

We are a small team that does serious work. If you are ambitious, intellectually rigorous, and drawn to South-East European markets, we would like to hear from you.

ALPHACAP Partners offers an environment that is genuinely different from a large investment bank. You will work directly with senior professionals on live transactions from day one. There is no padding of teams, no hierarchy for its own sake. If you perform, you progress.

We are particularly interested in individuals who combine strong analytical foundations with a genuine interest in the markets we operate in - Greece, Cyprus, and South-East Europe more broadly. Language skills and regional knowledge are always valued.

What We Value

What We Look For

Intellectual Rigour

We work on complex, often ambiguous situations. We need people who can think clearly under pressure and produce work of the highest quality without being told how to do it.

Commercial Instinct

Good financial analysis is necessary but not sufficient. We look for people who can translate numbers into insight and insight into advice a client can act on.

Regional Interest

Our focus on South-East Europe is a deliberate strategic choice, not a constraint. Candidates who share a genuine interest in the region - whether through heritage, language, or prior experience - thrive here.

Character

We are a small team and culture matters. We look for people who are honest, direct, and collegial - who take the work seriously without taking themselves too seriously.

Send Us Your CV

We do not always advertise open positions. If you are interested in joining ALPHACAP Partners at any level - from Analyst through to senior roles - send us your CV and a brief covering note explaining your interest.

All applications are treated in the strictest confidence.
