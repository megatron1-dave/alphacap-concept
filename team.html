Our People

The Team

Our principals have worked at some of the world's leading investment banks and private equity firms. Every client engagement is led at senior level - there is no passing of work down the chain.

Emmanuel G. Androulakis
Managing Partner

Emmanuel brings 25 years of expertise in Private Equity and Investment Banking, with a career spanning some of the most complex transactions across South-East Europe.

Emmanuel began his career at UBS Investment Bank in Equity Research, specialising in Metals & Mining, where he achieved top rankings as an analyst in both European and Global surveys from Institutional Investor and Reuters.

He subsequently progressed to UBS's M&A department, concentrating on South-East Europe for eight years, during which he led numerous equity, debt, and M&A transactions, gaining extensive experience in complex cross-border transaction structuring and execution.

He went on to establish and lead the Investment Banking department at Axia Ventures Group in Greece, developing it into the country's largest independent advisory team. He later joined the Libra Group in London as Executive Vice President for five years, completing multiple investments across energy, retail, financial services, telecommunications, hospitality, and real estate.

Emmanuel founded ALPHACAP Partners in 2016 with the conviction that clients in South-East Europe deserve truly independent, senior-level counsel of the highest standard.

Education
  • M.Phil. in Finance, St John's College, University of Cambridge
  • B.Sc. (First Class Honours) in Banking & International Finance, CASS Business School, London
Distinctions
  • Fellow of the Cambridge European Trust
  • Former Executive Committee Member, Hellenic Bankers Association, UK
  • Ranked No. 1 Analyst, Institutional Investor & Reuters
George Axaris
Vice President

George brings investment banking experience from two of the world's leading financial institutions, with a strong grounding in financial advisory and global capital markets.
